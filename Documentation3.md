# Name: 
donut()

## Examples:
donut(width/2,height/2,200)
Draws a donut in the middle of the window.

## Description:
Draws a colored circle then a black circle 1/3 its size

## Syntax:
donut(x,y,diam);

##Parameters: 
x - x coordinate of donut
y - y coordinate of donut
diam - diameter of donut

##Returns:
Displays donut
