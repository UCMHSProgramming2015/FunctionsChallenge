## Description:
with this function you can easily find the final velocity of something by inputting the inital
velocity, the acceleration, and the time

## Syntax:
 FinalVelocityOfObject (a, b, c);

##Parameters: 
a: initial velocity
b: acceleration
c: time

##Returns:
int
