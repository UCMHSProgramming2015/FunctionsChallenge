# Name: 
grav()

## Examples:
grav(25,r1,34,r2) = something

## Description:
gives gravitational force between two objects given their masses and positions

## Syntax:
grav(m1,r1,m2,r2);

##Parameters: 
m1: mass of 1st object
r1: position vector of 1st object
m2,r2: mass and position of 2nd object

##Returns:
float

##Other notes:
None
